# Target Jobs

Use this file to track high-fit roles before submitting applications.

## What To Capture

- company
- role title
- location
- required tools
- your matching tools
- domain fit
- why this role is worth the application

## Quick Filter

Apply when:
- at least 70% of the tool stack matches
- the role involves reporting, KPI tracking, operations, analytics, or stakeholder support
- healthcare, public sector, hospital, insurance, or health operations language appears in the posting
