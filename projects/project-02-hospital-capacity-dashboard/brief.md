# Project Brief

## Title

Canadian Hospital Capacity Dashboard

## Business Question

How can public hospital and health system data be used to monitor capacity pressures across regions?

## Industry

Healthcare

## Dataset Source

Government of Canada, provincial health open data, or city-level health system datasets

## Stakeholder

Healthcare administrators, health authorities, and operations teams

## KPIs

- Capacity utilization proxy metrics
- Regional comparison
- Monthly or quarterly trend changes

## Deliverable

Interactive dashboard in Power BI
