# Project Brief

## Title

Canadian Healthcare Wait Times Analysis

## Business Question

How do healthcare wait times vary across provinces, and where are the biggest trends or service gaps?

## Industry

Healthcare

## Dataset Source

- Statistics Canada Table 13-10-0701-01 Waiting times at selected percentiles for specialized services, by type of service, household population aged 15 and over, Canada

## Stakeholder

Hospital operations leaders, healthcare planners, and policy analysts

## KPIs

- Median wait time
- Percentile-based variation by service type
- Year-over-year change

## Deliverable

Analytical report with charts and summary insights
