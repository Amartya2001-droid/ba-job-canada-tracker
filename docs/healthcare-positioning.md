# Healthcare Positioning

## Career Story

I am building toward an entry-level Business Analyst role in Canada with a healthcare focus.

My portfolio is designed to show that I can:

- work with Canadian public datasets
- translate messy operational data into useful metrics
- present findings in a business-friendly way
- build dashboards and reports that support decision-making

## Why Healthcare

- Healthcare is one of the strongest and most relevant sectors in Canada.
- Public data availability makes it easier to build credible, location-specific projects.
- Healthcare problems are operational, measurable, and business-relevant, which is a strong fit for Business Analyst work.

## Portfolio Narrative

Project 1 shows how I analyze access pressure through healthcare wait-time patterns.

Project 2 shows how I think about healthcare access and service coverage using facilities and population data.

Together, these projects make the case that I can understand a Canadian domain, frame a business question, and communicate evidence clearly.

## Resume Direction

Use wording like:

- Built healthcare analytics projects using Canadian public datasets to analyze access, wait times, and regional service coverage.
- Developed SQL and Power BI workflows to clean data, calculate KPIs, and present insights for business decision-making.
